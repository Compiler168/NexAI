FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge monthly maintenance fees?

The prices listed are one-time setup fees for building the solutions. We do offer optional monthly retainers for ongoing optimization and API management, but you are not locked into them. You own the workflows we build.

How long does a typical implementation take?

Starter workflows typically take 1-2 weeks. Custom Agent deployments take 3-5 weeks depending on the complexity of your data. Enterprise projects are scoped individually.

Who pays for the OpenAI / Make.com API usage?

You do. We build the infrastructure on your own accounts (OpenAI, Make, Zapier, Pinecone, etc.) so you have complete ownership and transparency over your monthly usage costs, which are typically very low compared to human labor.
