Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with NexAI.

General & Services

What exactly does NexAI do?

We build custom artificial intelligence systems and automated workflows for businesses. Instead of you having to learn how to use AI or hire developers, we act as your dedicated engineering team. We audit your business, identify where AI can save you time or money, and then build, deploy, and maintain those systems.

Do I need to be technically savvy to work with you?

Not at all. We handle all the technical heavy lifting—from architecture and prompt engineering to API integration and deployment. Your role is simply to define the business problem and the desired outcome. We provide training for your team on how to interact with the finished systems.

Data Privacy & Security

Is my company data safe when using your AI agents?

Yes. We build primarily using enterprise-grade APIs (like OpenAI's API, Anthropic, or open-source models). By default, enterprise API usage is not used to train future foundational models. Furthermore, we can deploy solutions on your own cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP) to ensure data never leaves your environment.

Can you build HIPAA/SOC2 compliant systems?

Yes. For healthcare, finance, or enterprise clients requiring strict compliance, we design specific architectures utilizing zero-retention policies, dedicated vector databases, and compliant infrastructure providers.

Pricing & Timelines

How much does an AI project cost?

Our projects typically range from $2,500 for foundational workflow automation up to $25,000+ for enterprise-grade custom agent deployments. We price on a flat-fee basis per project so you know exactly what you'll pay upfront. Check out our pricing page for more details.

How long does it take to see results?

Time-to-value is one of our core metrics. Basic automated workflows can be built and deployed in 1-2 weeks. Complex RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) agents typically take 3-5 weeks to build, test, and deploy.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Reach out to us directly or schedule a free discovery call.
