Our Story

From theory to execution.

NexAI was founded in 2024 by former infrastructure engineers who saw a massive gap between the capabilities of AI models and how businesses actually operated.

While tech giants fought over model benchmarks, traditional enterprises were still doing manual data entry and losing millions to inefficiencies.

We built NexAI to bridge this gap. We don't train foundational models—we apply them. We build the agents, workflows, and logic that allow normal businesses to harness artificial intelligence.
