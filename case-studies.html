The Challenge

TechFlow's support team was overwhelmed with repetitive queries, resulting in a 4-hour average response time and decreasing customer satisfaction scores. Human agents spent 70% of their day resetting passwords and answering basic billing questions.

The Solution

Eliminating Manual Data Pipeline Errors

Less Manual Entry

The Challenge

GlobalTech's logistics coordinators were manually copying shipment data from supplier emails into their ERP system, taking over 200 hours per month and resulting in an unacceptable 8% data entry error rate.

The Solution

We built a multi-step automation workflow using Make.com and OpenAI's API. The system automatically parses incoming emails, extracts structured shipment data using an LLM, validates it against business rules, and injects it into their ERP.

The Results

Manual data entry time was slashed to just 12 hours a month (for handling exceptions). The error rate plummeted to 0.2%, eliminating costly shipping delays and paying for the entire project within the first 2 months.
